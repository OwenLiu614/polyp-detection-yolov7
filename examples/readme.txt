Videos encoded with H264

1. Geometry_nodes_examples.avi - Examples of changes that can be made to geometry nodes setup, to affect colon shape & polyp distribution
2. Shader_graph_examples.avi - Examples of chnges that can be made to shader graph nodes to affect object textures.

The above examples show manual adjustment of parameters, but for dataset used in the paper, all parameters were randomly varied in code on each frame iteration.

3. colonoscopy.avi - Sample video sequence for colonoscopy dataset, with no variation in parameters between frames
4. laparoscopy.avi - Sample video sequence for laparoscopic dataset, with no variation in parameters between frames
5. colon_randomisations.avi - Selection of frames from the colonscopy training dataset
6. liver_randomisation.avi - Selection of frames from the laparoscopy training dataset
